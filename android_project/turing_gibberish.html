Here is my android application:


TURING GIBBERISH

OVERVIEW:

Turing Gibberish is an Encrypting and decrypting android application. Here you can Encrypt your information using a key. And you can decrypt that information only using the right key. And you can store your secure information on your devices storage or other places.

OBJECTIVE:

Nowadays, Information is one of the most dangerous things in the world. In the future, it gonna more dangerous. You write down your confidential information somewhere, so anyone can easily see it. anybody can change the information. Now a day human brains are not safe. Some day ago I saw a news that In Bangladesh, A group o hackers just human brains. In America 6/7 years age human brains got hacked. So In the world haven't any safe place to store our data. If you need to store your information you need to encrypt this. Information security was our basic idea.

EXPLANATION:

Turing Gibberish is an Android application for encrypting/Decrypting data using Caesar cipher. Caesar shift is one of the simplest and most widely known encryption techniques. The present age is the age of information, so information security is very important. Here is our basic idea. To create this application, At first, we need to build an encrypting algorithm. we choose Caesar cipher and Java programming. I researched encryption techniques. then I find CAESAR CIPHER. I loved this cipher then we start to create the backend program. after lots of problems, we finished encrypting and decrypting the program. Then we start to create this application. On the first page, we import an animation. here we used Lottie animation. and here we use another picture of Turing gibberish logo. and give it a motion. in the java program, I used a handler to wait 5 sec to go to the new page. after 5sec will open the next . The next page is about the basic Overview of "Turing Gibberish". and here we give two buttons. one for "next page" and another for "about page". here we also use a timer for 10 seconds. Main Page Divided into Two-part, Input part and Output part. In the input part, Your need Two items (Original text or Ciphertext and key). one more thing you need to know: Encryption or Decryption. When you input an original text and Key then you need to select encryption or decryption then click on the finger_Print icon then your text will be ciphertext also called Secure text. Then you can copy the output or you will be able to create a text file to save your secure data. or you can share it on any platform. On the other side, you already had cipher data to convert it plain text. you need to know the private key. when you enter the right key and select decryption then you get plain text. otherwise, you will get the wrong information.

BENEFITS:

  • You can share your encrypted data with your friends and family or Others

  • Anybody can see your information but they can't understand this.

  • You can copy all Encrypted or Decrypted data with a single click.
